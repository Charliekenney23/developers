### Added
* Added View Longview Plan ([GET /v4/longview/plan](/api/v4/longview-plan/#get)) endpoint. You may now view the current Longview subscription plan. 
*Added Update Longview Plan ([PUT /v4/longview/plan](/api/v4/longview-plan/#put)) endpoint. You may now change the Longview subscription plan.

### Fixed
* A bug was fixed that prevented the re-creation of Managed Service labels that had previously been deleted.
* A bug was fixed that prevented domain updates ([PUT /v4/domains/{domainID}](/api/v4/domains-domain-id/#put)) for pre-existing domains without specifying `master_ips`.
